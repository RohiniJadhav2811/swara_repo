About Us

This restaurant is world famous in Pune for Veg, Non-veg.
Although it is on the outskirts of Pune, people travel all the way to this
location regularly just to taste the food here. Such is its popularity.
Quite a few options for vegetarians are also available. This is a three
storied building, and is usually filled. There are waiting at times.
They have a seperate parking area. The service is fast.
Prices are slightly on the higher side. They have clean washrooms on
every floor. They accept cash, card as well as NFC payment methods.
They charge by default for the mineral water even when you have not
ordered, one often tends to miss seeing the bill.
